Evera Labs is developing the world’s first DNA powered decentralized supercomputer. We utilize technologies such as quantum computing, blockchain, CRISPR and AI to optimize the efficiency of our network and to provide the world with the democratized engine for Web3.0.

Decentralized Bio-Quantum Supercomputer.

Relying on silicon based computing and storage technologies is futile given the rise of Web3.0. Our staple decentralized network utilizes spare computing resources from a distributed network of devices and enterprise-grade servers. Our blockchain microtransaction platform allows network members to directly profit without any middle men. DNA provides file storage and network compute power while our quantum computing architecture further bolsters computing efficiency. Evera Labs introduces the industry's first bio-quantum computing platform. The platform is faster, cheaper, and more secure than existing solutions.

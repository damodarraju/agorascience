Our mission is to democratize the internet of the future

Agora Science is a technology company that is building the computing and storage infrastructure for Web3.0. Businesses and institutions of every size in addition to individuals will use our platform to perform computations, securely store files and lead scholarly level research.

The potential of the Bio-Quantum Network.

Despite the fast growing nature of silicon based computing, alternative computing and storage mediums are needed to pave the way for Web3.0.

Quantum computing, a radically new and evolving technology is poised to bolster the efficiency and speed of computing.

As a result of quantum architecture’s inability to store data, we employ DNA file storage and computation resources to conserve time, space and money at a ludicrous scale.

The new enterprise network

Agora Science combines DNA file storage with quantum computing to democratize computing power and create a more sustainable decentralized network.

  • Decentralization

    At the core of our network is a decentralized supercomputing and storage platform that reduces costs, increases speed, and prevents fraud.

  • DNA File Storage

    Our novel DNA storage technology allows for greater security of files, reduction of storage costs and the ability to store massive amounts of data.

  • Security

    Our decentralized blockchain platform ensures the integrity of our users’ and network members’ data and privacy allowing for greater security.

Research rooted in open-access data

Our research is only as good as the data we have. We aim to maintain the world’s largest repository of research data and develop partnerships with leading data marketplaces.

  • Support for the developer ecosystem

    We actively support outside development as well as open-source projects and seek to develop partnerships with organizations, companies and institutions.

  • Data privacy and security

    Data running through our platform can be chosen to be made private at the request of the user. Nonetheless, our open data repository will remain active.

  • Therapeutics for Space Travel

    Our first network use case is set to be the development of novel genetically determined therapeutics for space travel and anti-aging.

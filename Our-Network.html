Blockchain Powered Scientific Decentralized Computing and Collaboration Platform

Agora Science provides the industry’s first Blockchain powered distributed and decentralized scientific computing and data collaboration system

  • A Need for a Decentralized Platform
  • The Agora Science Ecosystem
  • Incentivized Data Sharing, Decentralized File Storage and Market Applicability

Data security and privacy are of the utmost importance when handling sensitive information. Although cloud based computing models exist (AWS/Google Cloud), these platforms are centralized in nature and thus pose a grave security threat for businesses and organizations.

Agora Science altogether changes the way scientific compute and file storage tasks are organized and executed and aims to assert dominance in a blossoming market. By significantly substantially reducing computation and file storage costs, complex applications such as scientific simulations, machine learning models and enterprise-level applications become more accessible and democratized.

Agora Science connects computers, smartphones, servers and mining rigs in a peer-to-peer network, allowing individual users and organizations to utilize the compute and file storage power of other users’ machines.

Unlike existing decentralized solutions, the Agora Science network allows users to join the network as either data storage providers or computing providers with separate incentivization schemes. Moreover, the network is fine tuned for the scientific field as we are current developing a decentralized genome sequencing platform.

Specifically, we are currently negotiating a partnership with NASA to leverage our platform for the genetic discovery of new drugs for space travel utilizing artificial intelligence based methods. Moreover, a core feature of the Agora Science network is a dedicated Ethereum-based transaction system, allowing network members to be directly compensated for their efforts and for computing requesters to pay only for the price of their compute job.

Through establishment of an ethereum token, users are incentivized to dedicate both compute and file storage power to the Agora Science network. Unlike competing platforms, users may exchange granted tokens for compute power or blockchain powered file storage, therefore merging the two largest decentralized solutions in a single platform.

While also maintaining next generation security, Agora Science fosters open collaboration and data transparency. Users and organizations that allow their data to be freely accessible will receive discounted rates. Moreover, users are incentivized to contribute personal data such as genome sequences, electronic medical records and tax reports to growing repositories that may be used by all requesters.

The platform will also allow outside developers to create new decentralized applications on top of the growing network. Agora Science is shaping the scientific ecosystem of tomorrow and thus has a broad range of applicability, improving the productivity of biotech, healthcare and governmental agencies to name a few.
